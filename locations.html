Greetings, brave reader and fellow warrior of the dark! You’ve stumbled upon the Locations page of the Skul: The Hero Slayer Wiki — your portal to every battlefield, corridor, forest, and forgotten shrine that the little skeleton must traverse.

From the ash-strewn remains of the Demon King’s Castle to the glimmering dread of the Leiana Desert, these lands pulse with danger, secrets, and tragedy.
Whether you're here to chart your next route, uncover hidden bosses, or understand the rhythm of each zone, this is your sanctuary of knowledge. But beware — the scenery may be beautiful, yet every step you take could be your last.
